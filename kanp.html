Data Source

Live tab: the most recently collected positions, refreshed every 60 s while the page is open — the same dataset as below (near-real-time from the Pi on the home network, up to an hour old from the GitHub snapshot), labelled with its age. Session heat stays in your browser.
History Map & Traffic Study: one dataset, collected 24/7 by the Raspberry Pi (pi/README) and published hourly to GitHub, which the page reads over HTTPS from anywhere. On the home network it reads the Pi directly for full resolution (auto-connects when loaded from http://raspberrypi.local:8787/).
Charts: FAA VFR Sectional & Terminal Area (official FAA tile service) · Weather: NEXRAD via Iowa Environmental Mesonet.
