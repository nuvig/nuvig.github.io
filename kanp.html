Settings

Default data via airplanes.live, polled every 15 s while the page is open. Trails accumulate while the page is open and are colored by altitude band. Shared history is collected periodically by a scheduled GitHub Action (best-effort timing) and merged with observations from your own browser.

Local receiver note: works with dump1090-fa (/skyaware/data/aircraft.json), readsb, and tar1090 (/tar1090/data/aircraft.json). Data source settings are stored only in your browser. A plain-http receiver URL will be blocked by the browser when this page is loaded over https (mixed content) — open the page over http on your LAN, or serve the receiver over https (e.g. a Cloudflare Tunnel). See the receiver setup guide.
